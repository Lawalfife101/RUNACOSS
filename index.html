ABOUT US

Welcome to the Redeemer's University Association of Computer Science Students. We are a vibrant and dynamic community of students passionate about computer science and technology.

Our association strives to foster academic excellence, professional development, and a sense of camaraderie among computer science enthusiasts within Redeemer's University.

Our Mission

At RUNACOSS, our mission is to provide a platform for students to enhance their understanding of computer science concepts, engage in practical projects, and prepare for successful careers in the technology industry.

What We Do

  • Academic Support
  • Professional Development
  • Community Engagement

Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned computer science major or just starting your journey in the field, there's a place for you at RUNACOSS. Join us to connect with like-minded individuals, gain valuable skills, and make lasting friendships.
